My Books

Earthia Cold War

This book is about a brilliant alien from Mars who crash-lands in the Pacific Ocean in 1962, during the height of the Cold War. He is picked up by the USS Hornet and taken to the secretive, mysterious Area 51 in Nevada, where he finds himself mixed up in the Cold War. How will this affect the geopolitical situation? Will it make things better, or more complicated? The stakes have been raised.

Get your copy today and find out as you immerse yourself in a book that is both engaging and reminiscent of 1980s and 1990s-style Hollywood flicks.
